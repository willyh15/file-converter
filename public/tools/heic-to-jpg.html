Why convert HEIC to JPG?

Newer iPhones save photos as HEIC by default to reduce file size and store more pictures. HEIC is efficient, but many older apps, websites, and devices still expect standard JPG files.

Converting HEIC to JPG with GhostConvert helps when you need to:

  • Upload photos to forms and job applications that only accept JPG.
  • Share images with friends or family using older phones, PCs, or TVs.
  • Use photos in editors, blog platforms, or print shops that do not understand HEIC.
