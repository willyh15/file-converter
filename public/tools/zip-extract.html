What is a ZIP extractor?

A ZIP extractor unpacks compressed .zip archives into normal files and folders. ZIP files are commonly used to bundle multiple items together or shrink large folders into a single download. GhostConvert lets you extract ZIP archives directly in the browser without installing extra software.

  • Open ZIP files on devices without a built-in extractor
  • Unpack downloaded project folders, fonts, or templates
  • Expand email attachments that arrived as ZIP archives
  • Quickly view the contents of a ZIP before saving locally

How ZIP extraction works on GhostConvert

  1. Upload a .zip file from your computer or phone.
  2. The archive is scanned and unpacked into its internal folders and files.
  3. We rebuild a clean folder structure ready to download in one click.
  4. You download the extracted contents and use them like any normal files.

Common uses for the online ZIP extractor

  • Unzipping homework, course materials, or code samples
  • Opening downloaded design assets, fonts, or icon packs
  • Extracting website templates or WordPress themes
  • Unpacking multiple photos or documents sent as one ZIP

Frequently asked questions

Can I extract large ZIP files?

Yes, but extremely large archives may be limited by your browser and internet connection. If a ZIP is very big, consider splitting it into smaller parts or extracting only what you need.

Does this tool keep my files?

No. Files are processed securely and automatically deleted after a short period, as outlined in the Privacy Policy.

What about password-protected ZIP files?

Password-protected archives may not extract correctly unless decryption is supported. If your ZIP is encrypted, you may need to use a desktop tool that can prompt you for the password.
