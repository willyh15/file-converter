Why merge PDF files?

It is common to end up with several separate PDF files: one scan per page, multiple downloads from a portal, or different sections of a report. Merging PDFs turns all of those pieces into a single, organized document that is easier to email, upload, and store.

Common reasons to use a PDF merger:

  • Combine multiple scanned pages into one PDF packet.
  • Join separate chapters or slides into one document for clients or classmates.
  • Merge bank statements, receipts, or invoices for accounting or tax files.
  • Assemble a homework packet or multi-page assignment into a single upload.

GhostConvert keeps the process straightforward: upload, merge, download.

How the PDF merger works

  1. You upload two or more PDF files from your device.
  2. The files are combined in sequence into a single PDF document.
  3. Each original page stays in order, with no watermarks added.
  4. You download the merged PDF and use it anywhere you would use a normal document.

The goal is a clean, merged file that just works — no layout builder, no extra steps.

Best uses for a PDF merge tool

  • Students combining multiple PDFs into a single assignment upload.
  • Professionals assembling a proposal, contract, or slide deck from several parts.
  • Anyone merging scans from a basic printer or phone camera “scanner” app.
  • Organizing personal records like medical documents or insurance statements.

Tips for smooth PDF merging

  • Upload the files in the order you want them to appear in the final PDF.
  • Check that each source file opens correctly before merging.
  • Use the Compress PDF tool afterward if the merged file is large.

Frequently asked questions

Can I merge more than two PDFs?

Yes. You can upload multiple PDF files at once and GhostConvert will merge all of them into a single document.

Does merging change my original files?

No. Your original PDFs are not modified. A new merged PDF is created from the files you upload.

What happens to my PDFs after merging?

Files are stored only long enough to create the merged output and keep the download available briefly. After a short period, they are automatically deleted as explained in the Privacy Policy.

Files are processed securely and automatically deleted after a short period. GhostConvert is a fast, no login toolset designed to stay simple and useful.
