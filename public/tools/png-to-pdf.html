Why convert PNG to PDF?

PNG files are great for images and screenshots, but PDF is easier to share, print, and archive. Converting PNG to PDF lets you bundle related images into a single, simple document that opens the same way on any device.

  • Combine scans, photos, or screenshots into one PDF.
  • Prepare homework, receipts, and simple forms for email or upload.
  • Keep layouts consistent when printing or viewing on different devices.

GhostConvert keeps the process quick and minimal so you can go from loose PNGs to a ready-to-share PDF in just a few clicks.

How PNG → PDF conversion works

  1. Upload one or more PNG images from your phone, laptop, or tablet.
  2. GhostConvert orders the pages in the same order they were selected.
  3. The images are wrapped into a single PDF file using standard tools.
  4. You download a clean PDF that’s ready to send or print.

For quick multi-page documents, simply drag in all your PNG scans at once and convert them together instead of sending images one by one.

Tips for best results

  • Use clear, well-lit PNG scans for documents with text.
  • Upload pages in the order you want them to appear in the PDF.
  • Keep file sizes reasonable for faster conversion and downloads.

Frequently asked questions

Can I combine multiple PNGs into one PDF?

Yes. Select or drop multiple PNG files at once and GhostConvert will merge them into a single multi-page PDF in the order they are uploaded.

Will my images lose quality?

The images are embedded into the PDF at their original resolution. You may see some compression to keep file sizes reasonable, but documents remain crisp and readable.

How long are my files stored?

Files are kept only long enough to complete the conversion and provide your download link. They are deleted automatically after a short period as described in the Privacy Policy.
