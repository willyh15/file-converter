Why convert GIF to WEBP?

Animated GIFs are simple to share but they are heavy and use older compression. WEBP is a modern image format designed by Google that supports animation, better compression, and higher visual quality at smaller file sizes.

Converting GIF animations to WEBP with GhostConvert helps you:

  • Reduce file sizes for faster page loads and lighter chats.
  • Keep animations looking crisp on modern screens.
  • Use a format that is widely supported in current browsers and apps.

If you run a website or web app, switching looping animations from GIF to WEBP is one of the easiest ways to speed things up without redesigning anything.

Frequently asked questions

Will my animation still loop?

Yes. If your GIF is set to loop, the converted WEBP will also be an animated, looping image that can play repeatedly in compatible browsers.

Is WEBP supported everywhere?

WEBP is supported in all major modern browsers and many apps. For older environments that do not support WEBP, you may want to keep a fallback GIF or PNG as a backup.
