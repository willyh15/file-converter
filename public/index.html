What is GhostConvert?

GhostConvert is a lightweight, no nonsense file conversion toolkit for everyday use. Many converters are overloaded with pop ups, logins, and confusing options. GhostConvert focuses on the most common jobs people actually need: shrinking PDFs for email, turning phone photos into web ready images, converting JPG photos into clean PDFs, merging multiple PDFs into one, splitting large PDFs into smaller sections, deleting extra PDF pages you do not need, extracting audio from video, and making GIFs and ZIP files easier to handle.

The tools are designed to work well on slow connections, older laptops, and modern phones. You do not need an account, you do not need to install anything, and you do not have to fight through ads before you can download your file. You choose a file, pick your converter, and GhostConvert handles the rest in a few seconds.

Built for simple, repeatable workflows

  • Creators who need thumbnails, preview images, and smaller uploads.
  • Students sending assignments and scanned pages as a single PDF or just a few extracted pages.
  • People removing blank or extra pages from scans before sending them.
  • Small businesses preparing documents, invoices, and slide decks for clients.
  • Anyone who just wants a clean, fast way to convert, merge, split, or delete pages from a file without installing software.

GhostConvert will keep growing over time with more formats and small quality of life features, but the goal stays the same: clear tools, fast conversions, and a dark interface that stays out of your way.

How file conversion works on GhostConvert

  1. Pick the converter that matches what you need to do (for example PNG → JPG, JPG → PDF, Merge PDF, Split PDF, Delete PDF Pages, or MOV → MP4).
  2. Drop your file into the upload box or tap to browse from your device.
  3. GhostConvert processes the file using well known tools like ImageMagick, FFmpeg, and Ghostscript.
  4. When processing is finished, you get a clean download link with no extra clutter.

Files are handled for the sole purpose of conversion and are automatically cleaned up after a short period. The focus is on reliable, everyday utilities rather than gimmicks, so you can return any time you need a quick conversion job done.
